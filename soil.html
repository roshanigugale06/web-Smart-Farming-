Red Soil

Red soil is rich in iron and gives a reddish appearance. It is commonly found in southern and eastern parts of India.

  • Good drainage capacity
  • Low nitrogen content
  • Suitable for dry farming
  • Needs organic fertilizers

Black Soil

Black soil is also known as Regur soil and is rich in clay. It retains moisture for a long time.

  • High moisture retention
  • Rich in calcium & magnesium
  • Sticky when wet
  • Cracks during dry season
